Bnana. Designer Bananas

An exclusive online store that sells exotic designer bananas. Final Dev Academy team project inspired by our team's shared passion for bananas and the motivation behind starting the designer banana store was showcased in a video trailer. You can watch the trailer here.

Tech Stack Used |

My Role in the Team

Design, Front End and Style.

I played a lead role in designing, styling, and developing the website. The project started with the initial design phase, for which we used Figma, a popular design tool. In Figma, we created wireframes and mockups of each page, focusing on the overall layout, placement of elements, and visual aesthetics.

This allowed us to establish a creative direction and ensure a cohesive look and feel across the website. We carefully considered the user experience, ensuring that the design was intuitive and user-friendly.

Tools |

Front End

Building beautiful responsive pages.

During the development process, attention to detail was crucial. I ensured that the fonts, colors, spacing, and alignments matched the design specifications. This involved using CSS techniques such as flexbox and grid to create responsive and dynamic layouts.

My Reflections

What worked, how and where can I improve?

Team and human skills reflections.

Working with the team on the Bnanas project was an enjoyable and highly functional experience. We fostered a collaborative and supportive environment where everyone's ideas were valued and considered. Regular stand-up meetings played a crucial role in ensuring consistent progress and effective communication.

Our daily check-ins allowed us to share updates, discuss challenges, and align our tasks, keeping everyone on the same page. These meetings were instrumental in identifying and resolving any roadblocks or conflicts in our work. Additionally, our team embraced open and transparent communication channels, such as group chats and video calls, which facilitated smooth and efficient communication.

We were able to discuss ideas, seek clarification, and provide timely feedback, contributing to the overall success of the project. The positive and constructive atmosphere within the team greatly enhanced our productivity and made the collaborative experience truly rewarding.

Add more features?

Wishlist and Favorites: Add the ability for customers to create wishlists or mark favorite banana types, allowing them to save products for future reference or purchase. This feature encourages return visits and improves user engagement.

Social Media Integration: Incorporate social media sharing buttons, enabling customers to easily share their favorite banana types or purchases with their social networks. This functionality can expand the reach of the website and potentially attract new customers.

Checkout and Payment: Implement the checkout functionality to allow customers to complete their purchases securely. Integrate a payment gateway to enable online transactions, offering a seamless and convenient shopping experience.

Final thoughts.

In conclusion, the Bnanas project has been an exciting endeavor, allowing me to showcase my skills in design, front-end development, and responsiveness. Collaborating with the team was a positive and functional experience, with regular stand-ups and effective communication channels ensuring consistent progress and smooth coordination.

Overall, the Bnanas project has been a valuable experience but also an opportunity to collaborate with a team, learn new skills, and deliver a visually appealing and functional website. It reflects my ability to lead in design, styling, and developing responsive web pages. The project's success lies in its ability to provide an enjoyable user experience and serve as a testament to effective teamwork and continuous improvement.
