E-Commerce App Bnana üçå

Bnana is an e-commerce website selling exotic bananas.

For my final Dev Academy group project, I served as the Front-End and design generalist in a team of five. Our aim was to create a functional e-commerce site while also critiquing capitalism and designer markets. My role focused on figma and front end user interface and user experience.

In less than a week, we delivered a fully functional website. One of the key highlights of my learnings was successfully integrating our faux company branding throughout our app. This was achieved by using a combination of Figma and CSS. I also learned how to use the React library and Typescript to create a dynamic and responsive user interface.

Technologies Used: Typescript, React, SQlite (with Knex), Vite, HTML and CSS.

Web App Dungeons & Developers

Dungeons & Dragons Themed Board Game for Developers

Embark on a unique gaming adventure with Dungeons & Developers, a captivating board game infused with the spirit of Dungeons & Dragons, tailored specifically for developers. In this collaborative group project, our aim was to roll the dice, unveil humorous outcomes, and determine who among the players is the ultimate developer in the dungeon!

Serving as the Frontend Lead, I took on the role of hands-on developer, steering the technical decisions for the design and user interface of the Dungeons & Developers web app.

A standout achievement of mine in this project was infusing the app with a cohesive style that perfectly complemented its functionality. Drawing inspiration from Dungeons & Dragons character sheets, I crafted a layout that not only embraced the theme but also contributed to a delightful and engaging user experience.

This project showcases not only my technical proficiency in frontend development but also my ability to merge creative design with functionality to deliver an engaging and enjoyable gaming platform tailored for developers.

Technologies Used: React, SQlite3, TypeScript, HTML and CSS.

Website Build Park Supplies & Playgrounds

Architecturally designed play spaces

In my role, I took on the responsibility of managing and designing the official website for Park Supplies & Playgrounds. Working closely with the Managing Director, our goal was to create a seamless customer journey while showcasing the diverse range of products and services offered by the company.

Utilizing Figma, I crafted detailed wireframes to conceptualize the initial layout of the website. This step ensured a clear vision before moving into the development phase.

Teaming up with the company's external marketing agency, we translated the wireframes into functional page templates, laying the groundwork for content and copy integration.

Coordinating with various departments and clients within the company, I facilitated the creation of high-quality multimedia assets, including photography, videography, illustrations, and compelling copy, all tailored for seamless integration into the website.

This project was an exciting journey that honed my skills in frontend development, design collaboration, and content integration, showcasing my ability to deliver a holistic web solution.

Technologies Used: Figma, HTML and CSS.

Other Tools Used: Adobe Suite.

Product Website PlayBlox

NZ Made Movable Play Equipment early childhood play areas

PlayBlox, an innovative product line by Park Supplies & Playgrounds, focuses on providing high-quality movable play equipment for early childhood play areas. As the designated designer and developer, I played a key role in bringing this exciting new product to the online realm through the creation of its dedicated website.

Working closely with the Lead Playground Designer and Playground Consultant, we orchestrated a customer-centric journey that became the foundation for wireframing the initial layout of the PlayBlox website.

Leveraging Figma for initial wireframing, I transitioned into frontend development using HTML and CSS. This step ensured a responsive and visually engaging online platform, aligning with the innovative nature of PlayBlox.

Taking charge of the multimedia elements, I produced high-quality photography, videography, illustrations, and compelling copy. These assets were carefully curated to enhance the website's visual appeal and effectively communicate the unique features of PlayBlox.

This project was an exciting journey that honed my skills in frontend development, design collaboration, and content integration, showcasing my ability to deliver a holistic web solution.

Technologies Used: Figma, HTML and CSS.
