Learning outcomes Stage one

Figma, HTML and CSS

Along with developing a portfolio I also wanted to set some learning outcomes by refining my knowledge about Figma, HTML, CSS and an overview some of the popular frameworks that come along with these such as SASS and Tailwind. So with these goals in mind I organised complementary online courses and planned what specifically it was I wanted to refine and improve.

Tech |

Learning by making

Why, how and expectations

As a professional designer making a transition to software development and through my experience at Dev Academy, I feel presentation is really important. I believe a strong fundamental understanding of the tools necessary for visual presentation is quite important to me. Also design tools like Figma allow me to have another method to communicate my early ideas and concepts to my team visually. The building blocks of the web HTML and CSS are tools that I felt like I could improve on in terms of visual structure, responsive design, animations and features. I also wanted to learn more about best practice in terms of accessability, folder and file structures.

Using my lessons from my previous studies and work experience as my base to begin building my portfolio I would also supplement my any knowledge gaps with Skill Share Classes. Links to lesson below.

I want to come away with a strong understanding of the process of building from the beginning to the end on the front end. My goal is to feel confident in speaking about and offering my opinion about the design, code and structure of a website.
